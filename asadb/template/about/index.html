About

The ASA Group Database is provided principally for the use of the ASA Executive Board, MIT student group officers, current and potential group members, and MIT offices supporting those groups.

To contact the Association of Student Activities, please see our contact information. For issues directly about the ASA Group Database, please contact the database team at asa-db@mit.edu.

See also:

  • Use of Data
  • Information on visibility of account numbers (on the ASA website)

The database was originally developed by Alex Dehnert. Continuing maintenance has been by the ASA Executive Board, in particular Geoffrey Thomas and Rachel Meyer.
