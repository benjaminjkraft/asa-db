Membership confirmations — ASA Exec pages

Exec members can check the list of groups with issues (or a version with account numbers)

The list includes the following types of issues:

unsubmitted
active groups ("group status" is "Active") who have not submitted an update (ever — see ASA-#191)
confirmations
groups that have submitted an update (ever) but had less than five members confirm membership (again, ever — ASA-#191)
50%
groups that (ever) submitted a confirmation with more non-students (alum, other affiliates, or other) than students (grad or undergrad)

The technically-inclined may wish to read /asadb/forms/views.py to verify these issue types.
